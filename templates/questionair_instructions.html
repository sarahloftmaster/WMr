I will be presented with 2 symbols and have to pick one of them.

I am using the 1 key to choose the left symbol and the 0 key to choose the right one.

I will know ahead of time which symbol will be correct and which will be incorrect.

Any given symbol will always be correct or incorrect.

I will have to learn through trial-and-error which symbols have a higher chance of being correct.

The better I do, the faster the experiment will be over and the bigger my bonus will be.
